Tracking weight training hasn't evolved past pen and paper

Lift. Log. Lift. Log. Anyone who is serious about tracking strength knows this routine well. Not only is the task tedious, but it is frustrating to watch your numbers stay stagnant for weeks, even though you feel stronger.

Velocity based training is the future of lifting. For a price

The nmber of reps you do is not the only thing that matter, you also have to consider how easy and how fast you are able to complete each rep. This allows you to observe the small changes from workout to workout. Unfortunately, these systems can cost upwards of $10,000 (454h 33m).

The DataLift is the solution

The DataLift is a compact, inexpensive, and simple device that allows the user to see and record the amount of reps and velocity during a particular lift.

The press of a button transforms your workout.

There is no complicated set up necessary for use. Just rack your weight, clip on the DataLift, press a button, and lift.

How does it Work?

Using a photon, we send accelerometer data real-time to a node.js server with a TCP connection. Then utilizing Server-Sent Events, we forward the data our front end, and graph the results.

Uniquely, we have a advanced filtering algorithm that averages and records repititions.
